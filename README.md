# MPhil & PhD Lifecycle Management System

The **MPhil & PhD Lifecycle Management System** is a faculty-level, web-based platform designed to manage and track postgraduate research students throughout their entire academic journey. It replaces fragmented document handling and email-based coordination with a centralized, structured, and transparent system.

The platform supports the complete research lifecycle, including:
- Student registration
- Supervisor assignment
- Research proposal submission and approvals
- Periodic progress reviews
- Ethics clearance tracking
- Thesis submission and examination management 

Role-based access ensures clear separation of views and responsibilities for **postgraduate students**, **supervisors**, **coordinators**, and **faculty administrators**.

## 🌟 Key Features 

- **Secure Document Storage**: Offers reliable storage with version control for important academic documents.
- **Automated Milestone Reminders**: Provides deadline tracking and integrated notifications.
- **Audit Trails**: Maintains clear records and logs of all academic decisions.
- **Analytical Dashboards**: Monitors student progress and supervisor workloads interactively.

Built with long-term data consistency, compliance, and scalability in mind, the system aims to significantly **reduce administrative overhead** while improving governance and regulatory adherence within postgraduate research management.

## 🛠 Technology Stack

### Backend Platform
- **ERPNext** – Used as the core application platform to manage structured workflows, permissions, and academic processes.
- **Frappe Framework** – Utilized for customizing DocTypes, workflows, forms, and role-based access control to meet postgraduate research requirements.

### Database
- **Relational Database (MariaDB / MySQL / PostgreSQL)** – Provides reliable, structured storage for long-term academic records, research milestones, and audit logs.

### Frontend
- **HTML, CSS, JavaScript** – Used for interface customization and enhanced user interactions within the ERPNext framework.

### Authentication & Authorization
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** – Ensures secure access by assigning permissions based on user roles such as students, supervisors, and administrators.

### Deployment & Environment Management
- **Docker** – Used to containerize the application and its dependencies, ensuring consistent development, testing, and deployment environments.

### Version Control & Collaboration
- **Git** – Enables collaborative development, version tracking, and controlled code integration.
